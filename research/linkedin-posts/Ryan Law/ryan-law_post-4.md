# Post by Ryan Law — May 2026

does adding schema markup help your pages get cited in AI search? probably not 👇 

Louise L. and Xibeijia G. analyzed 6M URLs and found schema is more common on heavily cited pages

BUT that's probably correlation, not causation: schema markup is more common on pages with good SEO generally

so we tested 1,885 pages that actually added JSON-LD schema and compared their citations before and after

verdict: across Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, and ChatGPT, we saw *no measurable difference* in citations (or even a tiny negative effect)

schema matters for plenty of reasons in SEO and AI search, but adding schema to your pages is probably not some magic fix for improving your AI citations ✌
