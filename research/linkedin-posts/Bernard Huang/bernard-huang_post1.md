# Post by Bernard Huang - June 2026

AI is using our work, then sending us none of the traffic.

I’m not anti-AI. I use it every day. But I also run websites, so I’m seeing the damage up close.

Here’s what’s happening.

Publishers pay people to write guides, reviews, news, and other useful stuff. Real work. Sometimes weeks of it.

Then AI bots crawl the site, take the content, and use it to answer people’s questions directly.

The reader gets the answer without ever clicking the site that made it.

That means no visitors. No ad revenue. No money to pay the writers.

On one of my sites, I checked the traffic and more than 80% of visitors weren’t even people. They were bots scraping content.

And I’ve felt the hit myself. One of my sites went from thousands of readers to almost nothing. Not because the content got worse, but because AI started giving away the answers before people ever reached us.

AI companies are making billions from work they didn’t pay for.

The people who made that work are getting cut out. So where should we go from here?
